Employee Handbook

Welcome
Welcome to the company. This handbook provides an overview of our mission, values, and general employment guidelines. It is intended to help employees understand expectations and available resources.

Company Mission
Our mission is to build reliable, scalable software solutions that help organizations operate efficiently. We value transparency, collaboration, and continuous improvement.

Work Hours
Standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Some teams may operate on flexible schedules depending on project needs.

Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval. Fully remote roles are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to act professionally and respectfully. Harassment, discrimination, or unethical behavior will not be tolerated.

Communication
Official communication should take place through company-approved tools such as email, chat, and ticketing systems. Important decisions should be documented.

Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted twice per year. Reviews focus on goals, collaboration, technical skills, and professional growth.

Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to paid time off, sick leave, and public holidays as defined by local labor laws and company policy.

Security Awareness
Employees must follow security best practices, including using strong passwords, protecting credentials, and reporting suspicious activity.

Updates
This handbook may be updated periodically. Employees will be notified of significant changes.
