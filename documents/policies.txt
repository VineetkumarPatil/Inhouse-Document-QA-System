Company Policies

Data Privacy Policy
All employee and customer data must be handled securely. Access to sensitive data is limited to authorized personnel only.

Password Policy
Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Passwords must not be reused across systems.

Acceptable Use Policy
Company systems may only be used for legitimate business purposes. Installing unauthorized software is prohibited.

Incident Reporting
Security incidents must be reported to the IT team within 24 hours. Incidents include data breaches, lost devices, and unauthorized access.

Access Control
Access to internal systems is granted based on job role and reviewed quarterly. Employees must not share credentials.

Compliance
Employees must comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and internal standards. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action.

Termination Policy
Upon termination, all company property must be returned and system access will be revoked immediately.

Policy Enforcement
Violations of company policies may result in warnings, suspension, or termination depending on severity.
